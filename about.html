About StreamApp

What is StreamApp?

StreamApp is a simple, no-frills frontend for finding and watching movies and TV shows. Search for any title, pick a server, and start watching — that's it. No accounts, no subscriptions, no noise.

Movie & TV Data

All metadata — titles, posters, descriptions, and release years — is sourced from The Movie Database (TMDB). TMDB is a community-built movie and TV database. StreamApp is not affiliated with or endorsed by TMDB.

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

Video Playback

StreamApp does not host any video content. When you click "Open in New Tab", you are redirected to an external third-party video provider. StreamApp has no control over the content, availability, or quality of those external sources.

We recommend installing uBlock Origin in your browser for a cleaner experience on external sites.

Available Servers

  • Rakan — Best overall quality
  • Bard — Good for new releases
  • Xayah — Alternative for new releases
  • Ekko — 4K quality
  • Naafiri — 4K quality (alternative)

If one server doesn't work, try another. Availability varies by title.

Disclaimer

StreamApp is a personal project built for educational and informational purposes. It does not store, distribute, or host any media files. All video content is served by external providers. Use at your own discretion.
